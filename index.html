AI-PING-PONG





Instructions

  1. There are two sides to the Pong game, an orange, and a red side, you are the red side, since you are the Player.

  2. Using your mouse or touchpad, you can move the red paddle up or down.

  3. The goal of the game is to keep hitting the ball, by moving your paddle up and down against the computer.

  4. Each time you return the ball against the computer, the ball speed increases, making the game more challenging.

  5. If you lose the game, a prompt will tell you to reload the game and give you another chance at playing.

  6. Most Importantly, don't give up right away and have fun!
